How it works

Four steps to fix a road

Takes less than 2 minutes. No account needed. 100% anonymous.

01

Photograph the Pothole

Click 3 photos from different angles — front, side, and close-up. Use your camera or upload from gallery.

›
02

Pin Your Location

Allow location access for auto GPS coordinates, or type the area name. Location helps authorities find the exact spot.

›
03

Submit the Report

Your report with photos and GPS is emailed automatically to BBMP, Transport Dept, and BDA — CC'd to our admin.

›
04

Download Your Proof

Get a PDF proof of submission with a unique report ID, timestamp, coordinates, and department confirmation.

FIX BLR Roadmap — Make Bengaluru Great Again

✓

Phase 1 — Reporting Platform Live

Citizens can report potholes with photos, GPS, and get PDF proof. Authorities notified automatically.

2

Phase 2 — Public Accountability Dashboard

Track which wards have the most potholes. Score BBMP zones on repair speed. Live status tracking per report.

3

Phase 3 — Auto-escalation System

If a pothole isn't repaired within 30 days, automatically escalate to MLA, MP, and CM's office.

4

Phase 4 — City-wide Change

Partner with BBMP for direct API integration. Make Bengaluru a model smart city for civic infrastructure.
